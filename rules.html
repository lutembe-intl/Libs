Lutembe Boarding School

School Rules and Regulations

All students and parents must read and understand these rules before finalizing admission.

1. Fundamental Principle

  • 1.1 Common sense must be common. This is the guiding principle for all behavior within the school community.

2. Academic Conduct

  1. Language: English is the official language of communication at all times except during Swahili or Luganda lessons.
  2. Punctuality: Students must be in their respective classrooms, assemblies, or dining halls at the exact designated times.
  3. Preparation: Every student must attend all morning and evening preps (self-study sessions) without fail.

3. Personal Grooming & Uniform

  1. Official Uniform: The school uniform must be worn neatly and in full. "Slimming" of trousers or shortening of skirts is strictly prohibited.
  2. Hair: Boys must maintain a clean-shaven head. Girls must keep their hair short and natural or in simple, school-approved braids. No hair extensions or chemicals.
  3. Jewelry: No jewelry (earrings, necklaces, rings) or makeup/nail polish is allowed on school premises.

4. Prohibited Items (Contraband)

  1. Electronics: Mobile phones, radios, tablets, and cameras are strictly banned. Possession results in immediate confiscation and disciplinary action.
  2. Intoxicants: Alcohol, cigarettes, and illicit drugs are a "Zero Tolerance" offense leading to immediate expulsion.
  3. Food: Cooked food from outside is not allowed in the dormitories for hygiene and safety reasons.

5. Discipline & Safety

  1. Out of Bounds: No student may leave the school gate without a signed Gate Pass from the Headteacher or Warden.
  2. Relationships: Romantic relationships ("Coupling") between students are strictly forbidden.
  3. Respect: Absolute respect must be shown to all staff, prefects, and fellow students. Bullying or "teasing" will lead to expulsion.
