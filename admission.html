Rules & Regulations

  • "COMMON SENSE MUST BE COMMON"
  • Uniform: All students must wear the full, correct school uniform. Mixing with civilian clothes is strictly prohibited.
  • Prohibited Items: Mobile phones, radios (electronics) are strictly forbidden.
  • Language: English is the only language of communication. Use of vernacular or slang is an offense.
  • Punctuality: Students must be in class, at assembly, or at the dining hall exactly when the bell rings.
  • Dormitory Rules: No student is allowed in the dormitory during class hours or without permission from the Matron/Warden. Visiting dormitories of opposite sex is highly prohibited. Boys are strictly forbidden from entering the Girls' wing and vice versa. This attracts immediate expulsion.
  • Academic Integrity: Cheating in exams or failing to do assignments will lead to severe disciplinary action.
  • Property: Respect school property. Any student found damaging school property will be required to replace it with a new one.
  • Respect: Absolute respect must be shown to all teaching staff, non-teaching staff, and student leaders (Prefects).
  • Bullying: We have zero tolerance for "teasing" or bullying of new students. Offenders will be expelled.
